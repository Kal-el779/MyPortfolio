# Voody Kalpesh Rao - Portfolio

Welcome to my personal portfolio! This project showcases my skills in **AI / Machine Learning**, **Data Science**, and **Web Development** through an immersive, space-themed interface.

### 1. **Interactive Starfield**
- **Sucked into the Void**: A custom-built particle system where background stars are gravitationally pulled towards your cursor when you click and hold.
- **Constellation Network**: Connects stars with lines to form dynamic constellations as you explore.

### 2. ‚ö´ **"Gargantua" Black Hole Preloader**
- **3D Accretion Disk**: A fully 3D animated black hole inspired by *Interstellar*, featuring a golden accretion disk and a pure black event horizon.
- **Matter Absorption**: Watch as particles of light and matter are sucked into the black hole while the site loads.

### 3. **Modern UI/UX**
- **Glassmorphism**: Sleek, semi-transparent cards and sections.
- **Responsive Design**: Fully optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
- **Performance Optimized**: Uses `IntersectionObserver` for smooth scroll animations without lag.
